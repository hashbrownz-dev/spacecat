How to Play

In Space Cat, the objective is to reach the goal at the top of the tower.

Use the UP, LEFT, and RIGHT arrow keys to move Space Cat.
Avoid the GREEN OGRES.
Collect KEYS to open DOORS. Collect CATS for extra lives.
Collect COINS to become SUPER CAT.
SUPER CAT can break BRICK WALLS and DEFEAT OGRES.

Press SPACEBAR to Play!
